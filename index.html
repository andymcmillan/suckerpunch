Suckerpunch is a new alcohol-free bar in Portland, Oregon, offering a unique, curated menu of zero proof drinks.

Our menu consists of mixed drinks with alcohol-free ingredients, an extensive tap list including kombucha, shrubs, and sodas, as well as imported non-alcoholic favorites from around the world.

We'll begin with a weekly pop-up residency at Roseline Coffee, every Friday and Saturday evening, beginning in January. We're working to open our own permanent location later in 2020.
